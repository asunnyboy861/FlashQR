About FlashQR

FlashQR is a QR code display app that uses EDR (Extended Dynamic Range) technology to make your QR codes brighter and easier to scan. Simply add your QR codes, tap to display, and the app automatically maximizes brightness for quick scanning.

How to Use

  • Add a Card: Tap the + button to add a new QR code. You can scan with camera, import from photos, or type manually.
  • Display a Card: Tap any card to display it in full-screen mode with maximum brightness.
  • Auto Restore: Brightness automatically returns to your original setting after the timer expires.
  • Extend Timer: Need more time? Tap "Extend" to reset the countdown timer.
  • Manual Restore: Tap "Restore" to immediately return to normal brightness.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't EDR working on my device?

EDR (Extended Dynamic Range) requires an iPhone with an OLED display (iPhone X or later) running iOS 16+. If your device doesn't support EDR, FlashQR will automatically fall back to traditional full-brightness mode.

How do I add a QR code from my photos?

Tap the + button, then select "From Photos". Choose a photo containing a QR code, and FlashQR will automatically detect and extract the QR code content.

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

The free version lets you save up to 3 QR code cards with full EDR display. Pro unlocks unlimited cards, custom timer settings, clipboard import, and more features for a one-time purchase of $1.99.

Is my QR code data safe?

Yes! All data is stored locally on your device. We never collect, transmit, or store your QR code data on any server. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Go to Settings > Pro > Restore Purchases. Your Pro upgrade is tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device.
