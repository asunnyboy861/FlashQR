Privacy Policy for FlashQR

Last updated: May 7, 2026

Introduction

FlashQR ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

Data Collection

FlashQR does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All your data stays on your device.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • QR code content or images
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers
  • Camera or photo library content beyond what you explicitly choose to import

Local Data Storage

All QR code cards and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is never sent to any external server.

Camera Access

FlashQR requests camera access solely for the purpose of scanning QR codes and barcodes. Camera access is only activated when you explicitly choose to scan a code. We do not record, store, or transmit any camera data.

Photo Library Access

FlashQR requests photo library access solely for importing QR code images from your photos. We only access the specific image you select and do not scan or upload your photo library.

In-App Purchases

Pro upgrade purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Purchase verification is handled by Apple's StoreKit framework.

Third-Party Services

FlashQR does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

FlashQR is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data retention policy needed. All your data remains on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
