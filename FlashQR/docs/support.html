Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a QR code card?

Tap the + button on the main screen. You can scan a QR code with your camera, import from your photo library, or type the content manually.

How does EDR brightness work?

EDR (Extended Dynamic Range) technology makes the QR code area glow brighter than normal screen brightness on supported devices. This helps scanners read your code even in challenging lighting. On devices without EDR support, FlashQR falls back to traditional full-screen brightness adjustment.

How do I restore my brightness after displaying a QR code?

FlashQR has a triple restore guarantee: (1) Auto-restore timer returns brightness to your preferred level after 2 minutes, (2) Lifecycle restore activates when you leave the display screen, (3) Manual restore button is always available on the display screen.

What is the difference between Free and Pro?

The free version lets you save up to 3 QR code cards with full EDR display. The Pro upgrade ($1.99 one-time purchase) unlocks unlimited cards, custom auto-restore timer, clipboard import, and more. No subscriptions.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases". Your Pro upgrade will be restored from your Apple ID.

Does FlashQR collect my data?

No. FlashQR stores all your QR code cards locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
