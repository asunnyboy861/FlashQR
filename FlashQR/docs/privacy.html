Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

Information We Collect

FlashQR does not collect any personal information. All QR code card data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. No data is transmitted to any server.

How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no data usage to describe. FlashQR is a fully offline application.

Data Storage

All your QR code cards and app settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's built-in data storage frameworks. This data remains on your device and is never uploaded or shared.

Camera and Photo Library Access

FlashQR requests access to your camera and photo library solely for the purpose of scanning and importing QR codes. These permissions are used only when you actively choose to scan or import a QR code. No images or camera data are stored or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

FlashQR does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app is completely offline and does not connect to any external servers.

In-App Purchases

FlashQR offers a one-time Pro upgrade purchase processed through Apple's App Store via StoreKit. We do not have access to your payment information. All payment processing is handled by Apple according to their privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

FlashQR does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information at all, our app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
