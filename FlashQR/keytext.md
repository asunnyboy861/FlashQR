## Description
FlashQR makes your QR codes shine brighter than ever!

Using cutting-edge EDR (Extended Dynamic Range) technology, FlashQR displays your QR codes at maximum brightness, making them incredibly easy for scanners to read, even in challenging lighting conditions.

Why FlashQR?
We have all been there: fumbling at checkout, trying to show a QR code while the scanner cannot read it. FlashQR solves this instantly by automatically boosting your screen brightness to maximum when displaying a QR code, then restoring it when you are done.

Key Features:
- EDR Brightness: QR codes glow brighter than normal screen brightness on supported devices
- Auto-Restore Timer: Brightness returns to your preferred level automatically
- Quick Access: Tap any card to display it instantly
- Multiple Import Methods: Scan with camera, import from photos, or type manually
- Favorites: Pin your most-used cards for quick access
- Dark Mode: Beautiful in any lighting condition
- Triple Restore Guarantee: Timer, lifecycle, and manual button ensure brightness always returns to normal

Perfect For:
- Store loyalty cards
- Gym memberships
- Library cards
- Event tickets
- Boarding passes
- Any QR or barcode you need to show frequently

Free vs Pro:
Free: Up to 3 cards with full EDR display
Pro ($1.99 one-time): Unlimited cards, custom timer, clipboard import, and more

No subscriptions. No ads. No data collection. Your QR codes stay on your device.
